Welcome


On behalf of the Organizing Committee, we are pleased to invite the international scientific community engaged in research on Environment and Climate Action to attend the 1st International Conference on Environment and Climate Action 2025, which will be held during August 1-2, 2025, at Asian University for Women (AUW), Chittagong, Bangladesh. This conference encourages submission of research findings from faculty, researchers, and students from undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate levels.

Objectives


This conference seeks to advance transformative solutions by promoting women‚Äôs leadership in environmental sustainability, ensuring gender equity drives innovation and policy. We aim to facilitate interdisciplinary climate research and education, bridging gaps between science, policy, and practice to accelerate actionable knowledge. The event will showcase student-led and community-based conservation initiatives, highlighting grassroots efforts as catalysts for change. By building bridges between academia, civil society, and youth movements, we foster inclusive dialogue and collaborative problem-solving. Finally, the conference will create dynamic platforms for climate justice and biodiversity protection, uniting diverse stakeholders to scale up collective impact for a just and resilient future.

Venue


The conference will be held at the Asian University for Women (MM Ali Campus), Chittagong.
